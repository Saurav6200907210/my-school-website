Welcome to Our School website.

A wonderful vision in the name and form of St. Paul Public School, Teghra, Begusarai, came into existence in 2012 under the patronage of a great philanthropist Mr Sunil Kumar Singh after recognizing a genuine need to assist the children of Teghra who was compelled to travel to Begusarai and Patna for quality education because of the lack of a reputed school in Teghra. This is how the beautiful journey started. The school has been serving society successfully for 13 years and has never looked back ever since with Almighty’s care, blessings, and all-time support from all sections in and around Teghra. Brief Description of the School St. Paul Public School, Teghra, one of the leading CBSE-affiliated schools in Begusarai district, is deeply rooted in its philosophy of bringing the best in educational practices to its students. A school that places equal emphasis on scholastic and foundational domains to create empowered students who will not just be knowledgeable but also skilled to apply their knowledge meaningfully to create meaningful and positive changes in their lives. The school is solely committed to enriching the students learning with an innovative curriculum that is based on two strong pillars. Scholastic Domain- This includes the teaching-learning experience in subject areas relevant to the age group. There is a vertical progression and horizontal alignment to ensure that interconnectedness of knowledge is maintained, and students see knowledge as one body instead of fragmented pieces of facts and figures Foundational Domain- This includes four areas that are integrated with the scholastic domain and help the student develop the affective aspects of attitudes, dispositions, and attributes that will enable them to create a successful life for themselves and imbibe the qualities of a good citizen of the country and the world. These domains comprise: o Art Appreciation o Sports & Fitness o Citizenship Studies o Tech Integration Our school’s approach illustrates the inter-relationship of all disciplines of knowledge, promotes social and reflective thinking, and fosters the ability to face the myriad challenges of life comfortably. The technology-enabled classrooms coupled with interactive curriculum transactions, make learning a more engaging and joyous experience. Activity-based learning remains the core within which teachers weave storytelling sessions, fine motor and gross motor activities, language skills, and psychomotor skills. The tapestry of the curriculum is enhanced with trips and visits to local places. A peep into the classrooms would reveal an ever-smiling loving teacher, engaged students, fun, and frolic. Children love to come to school as their days are exciting. Each classroom is unique in its way. Here children are allowed to try, choose, change, reject, and discover their areas of interest and pursue them with fervor. We give children the freedom to make mistakes which boosts confidence and self-faith. We believe in nurturing compassionate individuals, who have a sense of justice, are global citizens, change agents who will in turn be good leaders rather than manufactured clones. Nurturing children into young, compassionate, and beautiful individuals is what we aim at. Learning is stress-free, fun-filled, and completely student-led. Children love coming to school as they find it a second home. There are a host of activities and inter-house as well as inter-section competitions through which we not only shape children into well-rounded personalities but also expose them to explore their likes and areas of interest.

Sports & Co-Curricular Activities

Sports & Co-Curricular Activities The purpose of these activities is to promote leadership quality in student. We intend to develop aptitude in students through active participation in cultural and literary activities like dramatics, debates, declamation contest and music contest. We do hold inter-house competition in sports, games, cultural and literary activities culminating in the annual sports and Annual Day functions. The School is creating a healthy atmosphere of inter-house competitions regularly. The school exposes the children to hobbies such as reading, Drawing, Dancing, Singing, Debating, Yoga and acting. In addition the school is providing the facilities of Kabaddi, Khokho, Basketball, Teble Tennis, Volleyball, Badminton and Carom Board etc. For kids, rhyming is taught on drum beating.
